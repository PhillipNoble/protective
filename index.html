What We Do

Flooring Installs Built
for Multifamily.

From a single turnover to a full property renovation, Protective Flooring delivers consistent, professional results — on schedule, every time.

Carpet Installation

Turnover-grade and upgrade carpet installed fast. We work with all major product lines and match existing specs across an entire property.

  • Full room and whole-property installs
  • Pad replacement and stretch repairs
  • Same-week scheduling available
  • Spec-matching across all units

Sheet Vinyl

The workhorse of multifamily flooring. Sheet vinyl for kitchens, bathrooms, laundry rooms, and utility spaces — fast and durable.

  • Full sheet and seam installations
  • Moisture barrier applications
  • Pattern matching and transitions
  • VCT stripping, waxing & buffing

Luxury Vinyl Plank

Waterproof, durable, and resident-approved. LVP for living areas, kitchens, bathrooms, and high-traffic corridors.

  • Floating and glue-down applications
  • Stair nosing and transitions included
  • Wide range of styles and price points
  • Subfloor prep and leveling

Tile Installation

Ceramic, porcelain, and stone tile for bathrooms, kitchens, entryways, and common areas. Set right the first time.

  • Ceramic and porcelain tile
  • Bathroom surrounds and floors
  • Grout cleaning and resealing
  • Backsplash and accent work

Subfloor Repairs

We fix it before we floor it. Damaged, soft, or uneven subfloors repaired by our own crew — no subcontractors, no delays.

  • Plywood and OSB replacement
  • Self-leveling compound applications
  • Water and mold damage remediation
  • Full documentation for your records

Pet Treatments

Odor and stain issues stopped at the source. Pet seal and enzyme treatments applied before new flooring goes down.

  • Pet seal treatment before install
  • Enzyme odor neutralization
  • Subfloor sealing and priming
  • Available as standalone service

The Company Behind the Work

We're Not a Franchise.
We're the Real Thing.

For nearly 50 years, Protective Services Company has been the flooring partner that Mid-South property managers trust when it matters most.

Our Promise

Founded in Memphis in 1976, we started as a small crew with a simple promise: show up, do the work right, and make the call when something goes sideways. That promise hasn't changed. What has changed is our reach — today we operate across Memphis and Nashville, serving apartment communities, senior living facilities, student housing, and commercial properties throughout the region.

We're not a franchise. We're not a national chain. We're the company that answers the phone, knows your properties by name, and has the crews, materials, and systems to keep your unit turns moving on schedule.
